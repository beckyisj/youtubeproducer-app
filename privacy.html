Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This privacy policy covers the tools and Chrome extensions built by YouTube Producer (youtubeproducer.app), including the YT Carousel Frame Capture extension and the YT Quote Carousel Generator web app.

What we collect

Our tools process the following data to function:

  • YouTube video content — The Chrome extension captures video frames (screenshots of the video player) and extracts transcript/caption text from YouTube videos you choose to process. This data is sent directly to the companion web app running in your browser.
  • Transcript text — Transcripts are sent to our server solely to generate AI-powered quote suggestions. Transcripts are not stored after processing.

What we don't collect

  • No personally identifiable information (name, email, address)
  • No browsing history or web activity tracking
  • No cookies or analytics trackers
  • No financial or payment information
  • No data from YouTube pages you don't explicitly process

How data is handled

  • Video frames are captured locally in your browser and never sent to any server.
  • Transcript text is sent to our server only for AI quote generation, then discarded.
  • No user data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.
  • No data is stored permanently on our servers.

Third-party services

The web app uses DeepSeek AI to generate quote suggestions from transcripts. Transcript text is sent to DeepSeek's API for processing. No other third-party services receive your data.

Chrome extension permissions

The YT Carousel Frame Capture extension requests the following permissions:

  • scripting — To capture video frames and extract transcripts from YouTube pages.
  • tabs — To open YouTube videos and monitor page loading during capture.
  • activeTab — To interact with the YouTube video player in the active tab.
  • Host permissions — To access YouTube for frame capture and the companion web app for communication.
